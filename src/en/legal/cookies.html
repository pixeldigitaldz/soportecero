Cookies Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your computer, mobile phone, or tablet when you visit them. They are used to make the website work efficiently, remember your preferences (such as light or dark theme), and provide information to site owners for statistical analysis.

2. What types of Cookies does this website use?

On SoporteCero we use the following categories of cookies:

  • Technical and Preference Cookies: Essential for the proper functioning of the website (such as remembering whether you have the light or dark theme active via local storage).
  • Third-Party Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense): Google and its partners use cookies to serve personalized ads to users based on their visits to this and other websites on the internet.
  • Analytical Cookies: Collect anonymous browsing data about user visits to improve loading speeds of tutorials and the overall portal.

3. Controlling Cookies in your Browser

You can allow, block, or delete cookies installed on your computer by configuring the settings of the browser you use:

  • Google Chrome: Settings -> Privacy and security -> Cookies and other site data.
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options -> Privacy & Security -> History / Cookies.
  • Safari: Preferences -> Privacy -> Block all cookies.

4. Consent

By using our technical portal and accepting our informational banner, you consent to the use of cookies under the conditions described above. You can withdraw or modify your decision at any time by clearing your browser history.
